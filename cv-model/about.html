Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have consistently leveraged artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems. Notably, I developed an AI-powered hospital monitoring system capable of detecting abnormal patient movements using pose detection algorithms like YOLOv8n, integrated into a Django platform. I also built intelligent data scraping tools deployed on Streamlit to automate information retrieval from the web. These projects demonstrate my ability to apply AI for both automation and healthcare innovation, combining algorithmic precision with impactful, user-focused solutions.

Understanding the critical role of cybersecurity in digital systems, I ensure that all my projects follow secure development practices. From securing sensitive patient data in the hospital monitoring system to managing certificate validation in a web/mobile application for Cameroonian councils, I implement user authentication, encrypted data storage, and access control mechanisms. My awareness of potential vulnerabilities and proactive design choices reflect a strong commitment to building resilient and trustworthy systems.

My work integrates IoT concepts, particularly in the context of healthcare and smart monitoring environments. The camera-based patient surveillance system exemplifies an IoT use case, where networked devices capture and transmit real-time data for AI-driven analysis. I also conceptualized a blood bank management system that could easily be extended to include IoT devices for tracking temperature-sensitive blood units or monitoring donation activity. These experiences illustrate my ability to blend hardware, data, and software into connected, intelligent ecosystems.
